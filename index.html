Lukáš. Kyjánek

ABOUT ME

My name is Lukáš Kyjánek and I am a full-time Master student at the Institute of Formal and Applied Linguistics, Faculty of Mathematics and Physics of the Charles University.

My fields of interest are derivational morphology and dialectometry with focus on mutual intelligibility of closely related languages.

E-mail: L.kyjanek@gmail.com
